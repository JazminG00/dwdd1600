Sylvanian Sets

What are the different set types?
Families, Boat Sets, Car Sets, Figure Sets, Keychains, Playsets, Accessory, Furniture, and Limited Edition sets.

While all of these different types of collectibles are great. By far the best one in my opinion is the keychain sets. That way you are able to carry around a little Sylvanian as a cute little trinket.

Deep Dive: More on First Sets!

Babblebrook Family
Crystal is the mom of the Babblebrook family, she also happens to be the best cook! Rocky is the father of the family, he is so charismatic that others say he should be a cars salesman. Oldes son Richard is their fist born who wants to be a tree surgeon. While Richard is more quiet, his baby sister Coral can get really noisy
Timbertop Family
The Timbertop Family is a household of brown bears that consist of: Father Taylor, Mother Rose, Birch, Ivy, Bud, Daisy, Burl, Blossom, Grandpa Gus, and Grandma Fern.
Wildwood Family
The Wildwood family is a group of Brown rabbits: Father Herb, Mother Ginger, Ray, Rusty, Hollie, Barkley, Juniper, Grandpa Smokey, and Grandma Flora.
