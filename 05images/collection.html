How Is It Considered Endangered

Critically Endangered
When as a species you have declined 25% in the span of three years. This can also be counted at having less than 250 mature aniamls in that species.
Endangered
Endangerment is weighed when a group as gone down between 50 and 70 percent.
Vulnerable
Vulnerability is calcualted over a span of 10 years, which consists of a decline of 30 to 50 percent.
