Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Decleration of Independance

Thomas Jefferson
The principal author of the Declaration and a future U.S. President.
John Adams
A leading advocate for independence, he later became the second U.S. President.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned diplomat, inventor, and statesman.
John Hancock
The President of the Continental Congress, known for his bold signature.
Samuel Adams
A key leader in the American Revolution and a cousin of John Adams.
Richard Henry Lee
The Virginian who proposed the resolution for independence.
Roger Sherman
A lawyer and politician who helped draft the Declaration.

These individuals were among the 56 signers who represented the 13 American colonies in declaring independence from Britain in 1776. Each of them played a vital role in the formation of the United States.
